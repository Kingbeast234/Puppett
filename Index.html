📖 Puppet 11.0 — Rules Guide

Objective — Three Win Conditions

  • ⚔ Collapse (C!) — Destroy the enemy Tower, or threaten it inescapably.
  • 💀 Elimination (E!) — Destroy every enemy unit and exhaust their reserve.
  • 🏛 Domination (T!) — Control the most territory when the game clock expires (15, 25, 30, or 60 min depending on mode).

Turn Order

P2 Black goes first each game. Each turn has four phases: Deploy → Move/Attack → Deal/Alliance. You may end any phase early.

Movement

  • Units move in the direction they face. Turn costs your action for that unit.
  • Ortho units move in a straight line. Fwd-cone units (Horseman, Raider, General) move forward + two front diagonals.
  • Clicking a green cell in a different direction auto-turns the unit and spends the action.

Army Groups & Protection

  • Units of the same type adjacent to each other form a group. A General shields any adjacent friendly.
  • Orthogonal attacks blocked if the target has at least one side-by-side ally on a perpendicular flank.
  • Diagonal attacks blocked if the target has an ally in either of the two orthogonal "component" cells that flank the diagonal approach — this protects ALL inner diagonal gaps between connected units.
  • Weak spots: only the outermost diagonal flanks of the end units in a formation are open. Archers and Raiders are the primary units that exploit these.
  • A stranded unit (alone, no adjacent shield) is killed instantly on any hit.
  • Gap corruption: enemy units blocking gap diamonds corrupt deployment routes only. Attackers do not advance into killed cells — no formation-entry immobilization.
  • Giant wall-break — Giants ignore army group protection entirely; they can attack through any formation.
  • Giant immunity — Only the direct front cell is immune. Sides, diagonals, and rear are all vulnerable.

Deploying Units

  • Roll income first — 2D6 per miner, once per deploy phase. Once you deploy any unit, rolling is locked for that turn.
  • Deploy within the 8 cells surrounding any of your existing units.
  • No Miner per-turn limit — deploy as many as you can afford (max 5 active at once). Reserve is limited — units are gone when eliminated (except General).

Territory & Gaps

The small diamond shapes between octagons are territory squares. Each unit you occupy claims all 4 surrounding gap squares. The player controlling more gaps wins Domination.

Deal Phase

Each turn ends with a Deal phase. Propose bead wagers — your opponent can accept or reject. Holding center rows 7 & 8 gives +20% on accepted wagers.

Timers

Each player has 1 minute per turn. When it expires, the opponent can click 📣 Call Clock to grant extra seconds — in Home Play this resets the clock with no penalty; in Default/Tournament it grants +15s then ends the turn. The shared game clock (15–60 min depending on mode) triggers Domination scoring when it hits zero.
